Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: 23 APRIL 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ntivo EdTech (Pty) Ltd ("Company", "we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Langa Workspace and our website (ntivoedtech.co.za).

2. The Data We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide and improve our educational AI services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and school affiliation provided during registration.
  • Usage & Prompt Data: The text inputs, curriculum selections, and prompts you provide to our AI tools (including Nano and the Lesson Planner) to generate educational content.
  • Technical Logs: Standard server logs, IP addresses, and browser types for security and debugging purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and secure the Langa Workspace platform.
  • To generate your requested lesson plans, assessments, and AI responses.
  • To analyze usage trends (e.g., measuring the speed of assessment generation) to improve our systems.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Artificial Intelligence Processing

Ntivo EdTech utilizes advanced cloud infrastructure and AI models (such as Google Vertex AI). Your prompts and curriculum inputs are processed to generate immediate outputs. We do not use your personal data to train public foundational AI models.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, utilizing secure cloud infrastructure hosted regionally to ensure data sovereignty and compliance.

6. Your POPIA Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You may exercise these rights by contacting us.
